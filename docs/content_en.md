2. Investor Pitch текст (как у mining компаний)
Strategic Graphite Project
KAZGEOMINER is developing a graphite exploration project located in Central Kazakhstan within the Northern Balkhash metallogenic province.
The region hosts favourable geological conditions for the formation of crystalline flake graphite deposits associated with metamorphosed carbonaceous sediments and graphitic schists.
Preliminary laboratory analyses confirm graphite content exceeding 40% carbon, indicating strong potential for the discovery of economically viable graphite mineralisation.
The project benefits from:
• favourable geological setting
• access to infrastructure
• proximity to international transport corridors
• growing global demand for battery-grade graphite
KAZGEOMINER is currently seeking strategic investors and industry partners to support the next phase of exploration and project development.

5. Страница MARKET
Global Graphite Demand
Graphite is classified as a critical mineral by multiple governments due to its essential role in advanced technologies and the energy transition.
Key applications include:
• lithium-ion battery anodes
• electric vehicles
• energy storage systems
• refractories and metallurgy
• lubricants and industrial materials
Demand for natural graphite is expected to increase significantly due to rapid growth in the electric vehicle and energy storage industries.
